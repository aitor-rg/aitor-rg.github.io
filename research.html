MARitime Internet of Things (MARIOT) Project

Intercompany collaborative project. See list of partners.

Simulation of satellite constellation network in Python. Modeling satellite kinematics for elliptic orbits, antennae budgets and communication protocols.

SafeEYE Project

Drone Research Lab, Aalborg University.

Design of an automated emergency landing system for big drones (>7kg) using two Neural-Networks (NN). One trained to detect flat areas of grass where the drone could potentially land, and another to detect increased vibrations emerged from faults in the system.

Safe insulin intensification in Type 2 Diabetes

Aalborg University, in collaboration with Novo Nordisk.

Stochastic modeling of T2D patients. Robust control for titration process using Model Predictive Control and output disturbance rejection for offset compensation. Fault-detection of glucosemeter faults and insulin missed doses.
