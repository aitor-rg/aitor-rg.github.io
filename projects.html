[q2-2018] Autonomous Control for Rendezvous and Docking of CubeSats

Master's thesis, Aalborg University.

Summary: A project I developed focusing on the control and navigation aspects of rendezvous and docking tailored for CubeSats. The project employed techniques such as quaternion-based Unscented Kalman Filter for state estimation, sliding mode control for attitude control, and model predictive control for the approaching phase. As for the actuators, I utilized reaction wheels and magnetorquers to manipulate the CubeSat's orientation, and startrackers and camera-based sensors to determine the spacecraft's inertial and relative position and orientation. This comprehensive project showcased my expertise in spacecraft control, navigation, and sensor integration for precise maneuvering and docking operations.

Keywords: Quaternion-based uncented Kalman filter, Sliding-mode control, Model predictive control.
