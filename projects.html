Autonomous Control for Rendezvous and Docking of CubeSats [q2-2018]

Master's thesis, Aalborg University.

Summary: This comprehensive project showcased my expertise in spacecraft control, navigation, and sensor integration for relative maneuvering and docking operations. The scope was tailored for typical and inexpensive CubeSat sensor and actuation equippment, and involved techniques such as quaternion-based Unscented Kalman Filter and sliding-mode for attitude estimation and control, model predictive control for the approaching phase, and camera-based sensors for relative state estimation.

Keywords: Quaternion-based uncented Kalman filter, Sliding-mode control, Model predictive control.
