Hello, World!

Welcome to my portfolio. My name is Aitor. I am an engineer born and raised in Barcelona (Spain) with a passion for physics and space exploration. I specialized in control theory at Aalborg University (Denmark), where I am conducting my Ph.D. research in guidance, navigation and control of spacecrafts and spacecraft safety.

I am also an avid reader of science-fiction and fantasy, my all-time personal favorite being Speaker for the Dead, by Orson Scott. In my spare time, one can often find me covering songs on the guitar and the piano, playing video-games and, occasionally, out in the wild climbing, scuba diving and being awesome!
