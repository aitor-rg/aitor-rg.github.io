Hello, World!

In this site you can learn about my humble professional achievements as a control engineer.

Barcelona, Catalonia

This is the place where I grew up and became an Engineer. I graduated with a B.Sc. degree from Barcelona School of Industrial Engineering at the Polytechnic University of Catalonia (UPC). Throughout my studies, I came across several engineering disciplines, yet one in particular matured solidly into a passion of mine, i.e. Control Theory. Only after my stay at the Kinematics and Robot Design Lab in the Institute of Robotics and Industrial Informatics (IRI) modeling and controlling parallel robots, my research took off.

Aalborg, Denmark

In Denmark I had the chance to quench my thirst for knowledge in a project-based learning education. I graduated with a M.Sc. degree in Control and Automation from Aalborg University (AAU), where I later became a Research Assistant in the Drone Research Lab. I currently conduct my research as a PhD fellow in Control, neighbouring two other passions of mine: Space Exploration and Space Situational Awarenes. My main contributions are thus, across the GNC and the Spacecraft Collision Avoidance systems.
