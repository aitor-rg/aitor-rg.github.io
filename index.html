BS @ Barcelona Technical School of Industrial Engineering

Barcelona is the place I grew up. I obtained my Bachelor's degree from the Barcelona School of Industrial Engineering, and conducted my thesis at the Institute of Robotics and Industrial Informatics, a joint research center of the Spanish National Research Council (CSIC) and the Technical University of Catalonia (UPC). Meanwhile, I teached Linear Algebra and Differential Equations to fellow students at a reinforcement university center.

MS / PhD @ Control and Automation Section of Aalborg University

I obtained my Master's in Control and Automation at Aalborg University/Denmark, where I specialized in spacecraft Guidance, Navigation and Control (GNC). Immediately after, I pursued my Ph.D. degree focused on enhancing spacecraft safety. Currently, I am working as a Postdoc collaborating with several companies in ESA-funded projects with the aim of developing GNC algorithms for launchers.

Visiting Scholar @ Mechanical and Aerospace Department of SUNY

During my Ph.D. studies, I was honored with the opportunity of visiting the Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering Department of the State University of New York at Buffalo under the supervision of Prof. John Crassidis, a SUNY distinguished professor and amazing mentor.

MARIOT WEBSITE 🡥

Design, construction and testing of an automated flight termination system for big drones (>7kg). This includes the implementation of neural-networks for 1) classification of landable ground areas and 2) structural vibration monitoring; and the formulation of a decision function to terminate the flight. Airborne tests were conducted, as well as lab tests using VICON motion capture system on a DJI M800 to gather nominal and faulty vibration data.
