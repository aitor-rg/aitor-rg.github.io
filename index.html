BIO

Artificial satellites are rapidly becoming a fundamental part of our future. Yet, as the space environment gets increasingly congested and contested, many aspects of space are still unregulated, posing a threat to all those assets we now depend on for navigation, communication, weather forcast, and more.

Sustainable development has proven to be the key to withstand the growing complexity of the world. Achieving sustainability in the space environment, as in any other field, is not an endeavor of a single individual but rather a cooperative effort that spans across multiple disciplines.

As a GNC Engineer, and a space exploration enthusiast, I am devoted to the development of algorithms and technology for the sustainable progress of the space industry. I value collaborative learning to the highest degree, recognizing it as the most effective means of acquiring knowledge and fostering innovation, and I believe in sustainability as the driving force for progress.

Aitor R Gomez, PhD
